✦ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is NeoBank safe and regulated? ▾
Yes. NeoBank is FDIC insured up to ₹250,000, PCI DSS Level 1 certified, and regulated by financial authorities in all operating countries.
How long does account opening take? ▾
Most accounts are verified and opened within 2–3 minutes through our AI-powered KYC process. No branch visit required.
Are there any hidden fees? ▾
Never. Our pricing is 100% transparent. What you see is what you pay. International transfers use the real exchange rate with no markup.
Can I use NeoBank outside my country? ▾
Absolutely! NeoBank works in 40+ countries. You can hold and spend in 30+ currencies with your physical or virtual card anywhere in the world.
How does expense tracking work? ▾
Every transaction is automatically categorized using AI. You get a real-time breakdown of spending by category, merchant, and time period.
How do I cancel my subscription? ▾
You can cancel anytime from your account settings with one click. No cancellation fees, no questions asked. Your free account remains active.
